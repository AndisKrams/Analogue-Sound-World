Analogue synths are considered the 'holy grail' for most producers and keyboard players, with most offering a unique sonic 'character' not found on their digital or sample-based counterparts. Monophonic (Mono) synths only allow for one note to be played at once, great for bending lead sounds and complex solo parts, with Polyphonic (Poly) allowing for multi-voice chords and pads to be expressed at the same time. Utilising analogue circuits and analogue signals to generate pure musical tones, these Synthesizers are extremely fun to play and tweakable in real time, with a vast array of presets and sounds already pre-built in for you to edit.
